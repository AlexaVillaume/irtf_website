The IRTF Spectral Library

The Extended IRTF Spectra Library consists of 284 stars spanning 0.7-2.5 microns observed on the medium-resolution infrared spectrograph, SpeX, at the 3.0 meter NASA Infrared Telescope Facility (IRTF).
